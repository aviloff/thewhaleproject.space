Alexander Yushkov

Composer, Music Producer

Alexander Yushkov is a composer and electronic music producer. His sound is recognized by silky analog synthesizer timbres, submerging atmospheres and deviant melodies.

Projects


  • 2016-2018 – LAYAH band – keys, sampling, backing vocals, live arrangements production (Live videos, Atlas Weekend Festival videos, Photos, Social page)
  • 2015 – Stas Koroliov band – music co-production, keys, drum machines, backing vocals, effects programming (Live video, Original remix production, iTunes release, Social page)
  • 2013-2014 – keyboardprayer – live electronic solo performer (Live videos)
  • 2010-2013 – soundtrack author for 'Yes, We Caught That!' game for Molyjam International Gaming Contest
  • 2013 – Dom Pionerov band – cofounder, music co-author, mixing (Bandcamp release, Social page)
  • 2013 – starring, soundtrack production, video editing of 'Null Movie' trailer for 'Null' LP by Widiwava band (Movie, LP)
  • Early 2013 – Midiwava concept – finger drumming, synths (Live video, TV appearance)
  • 2011-2013 – Widiwava band – music co-production, mixing, keys, sampling, effects programming (Photos, Live video, EP, Album, Social page)
